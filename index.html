About Me

Cheng Wang

Ph.D. in CS, KAUST

I'm currently a Ph.D. Researcher in Computer Science, working in the Visual Computing Center at KAUST. My research advisor is Helmut Pottmann. Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree in Automation at Tsinghua University, in 2016.

My research interests are Geometry Processing, Computational Design and Fabrication. My current research direction is fabrication-aware geometric design of constrained quad meshes, e.g. for applications in architectural structures.
