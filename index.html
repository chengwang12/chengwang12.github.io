About

Academic Profile

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computing Sciences at the University of East Anglia (UEA), UK.

I completed my PhD in Computer Science at KAUST with Professor Helmut Pottmann. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Automation from Tsinghua University.

My publications include work in ACM SIGGRAPH, ACM Transactions on Graphics, Computer-Aided Design, and Eurographics.
